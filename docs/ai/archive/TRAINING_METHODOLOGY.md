# TRAINING METHODOLOGY & LOGIC

## Vision
The app serves as an elite, intelligent backend coach. It blends proven scientific training methodology (hypertrophy, strength, and endurance) with cutting-edge AI adjustments.

## 1. Programming & Context Integration
- **Context is King:** The programming MUST be driven by the user's specific inputs (training history, available days, and exact equipment). If a user says they only have a barbell and a rack, the program cannot prescribe machine lateral raises.
- **Flexibility:** Users must be able to:
  - Tweak or edit pre-populated exercises.
  - Change the days they train dynamically without breaking the mesocycle.
  - Update their equipment access (e.g., "I'm traveling and only have dumbbells today").

## 2. Progressive Profiling & Benchmarking
- We do not ask the user for 100 data points on day one.
- **The Benchmark Week:** The first week of workouts (weights + cardio) is used to verify and benchmark user estimates. The app observes their actual performance to calibrate their 1RMs and initial volume landmarks (MEV/MRV).

## 3. The Hybrid Modalities & Granular Tracking
- The system must track granular, scientifically sound metrics across entirely different energy systems.
- **Hypertrophy & Strength:** Track Total Tonnage, Sets per Muscle Group per Week, and RIR / RPE drop-offs across the microcycle.
- **Endurance & Conditioning:** Track Zone 2 steady-state (Duration & Average HR), VO2 Max intervals (Pace & Peak HR), and MetCons (Cross-training AMRAPs/For Time).
- **Rucking & Tactical:** A dedicated tracking matrix for Rucking (Distance, Pack Weight, Elevation Gain, and completion time). The AI must understand the crushing systemic fatigue generated by a heavy ruck and adjust the subsequent day's lifting volume accordingly.

## 4. The Anthropic "Backend Coach" Integration
- Anthropic's Claude is integrated into the backend as a specialized "Coach" agent.
- **Role:** Every week, the Coach reviews the user's logged data across ALL modalities (lifting fatigue + cardiovascular load).
- **Action:** It verifies alignment with the underlying training methodology. For example, if the user completed a grueling 12-mile ruck on Sunday, the Coach will intelligently suggest stripping the heavy spinal loading (e.g., Heavy Squats) from Monday's leg session, pivoting to machine work to protect the CNS.

## 5. Free vs. Paid Tiers
- **Free Version:** Relies on robust but static templates based on the user's equipment profile.
- **Paid Version:** Uses the AI specifically to dynamically swap out exercises and entirely customize the mesocycle down to the individual workout based on ongoing adaptations.
